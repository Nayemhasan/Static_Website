How many glasses of wine cause you to get drunk?

There is no ‘one size fits all’ reply. Wine consumption / getting drunk depends on:
1. Glass size (or rather how much wine in the glass)
2. Alcohol contents (8% ? 16?)
3. Time frame (1 hour? 12?)
4. Time of day (I get tipsy much quicker drinking during day (especially in the sun) as opposed to evening)
5. How much food I had that day (typically people get drunk much quicker on empty stomach)
6. Mood (depressed people tend to get drunk quicker)
7. Bad vs good drinking day
8. Heritage (people from ‘alcohol drinking countries’ can drink much more without getting drunk than people from ‘dry countries’)
So how many glasses of wine would get me drunk? When I feel I am getting tipsy I stop drinking. (Control freak). To ge drunk I need to drink anything from a couple of glasses (or even one when consumed on a tropical beach) on a bad day to (on very rare occasions) a bottle on a good day.
Hence, the answer is anything from 1 glass (and a bit) to 1 bottle (and a bit)
